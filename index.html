WHAT IS ELON RANGO?

ElonRango is a deflationary currency built for the community. Each time a transaction takes place within Elonrango, 5% of that transaction is removed or “burned” from the total supply. This thus increases the scarcity of every Elonrango in circulation. In addition, another 5% is distributed among every Elonrango diamond hands. Finally there will be no more Elonrango in circulation than there is now so strap your bags and get sent..

Step 1: Install Metamask for Chrome, Android or iOS

Go to App Store or Play Store install the Metamask app. Follow the steps from their guide to set up a wallet.

Step 2: Send BSC (Binance Smart chain) to Metamask

Set up your wallet to receive and send payments on the BSC (Binance Smart chain) network. Set up a new network with the following data: Name: MAINNET BSC, URL RPC: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/ ID: 56 SYMBOL: BNB URL: https://bscscan.com/.

Step 3: Connect to Pancakeswap

Go to https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/ and click the “Use Pancakeswap” button. When Metamask asks for your signature, go ahead and sign it.

Step 4: Swap your BSC for ELON RANGO

Now you can swap your BSC for ELON RANGO. Set your slippage to 5-7% and buy an even amount of ELON RANGO. Add your desired BSC amount and the correct ELON RANGO amount will be calculated. Click on the “Swap” button to finalize the trade. make sure to set “fast transaction speed” in Metamask.
